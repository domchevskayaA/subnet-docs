Text-to-Speech (TTS) models serve as the backbone for a wide range of applications, from virtual assistants to audiobooks. MyShell pioneers this field by open-sourcing the best TTS models, OpenVoice and MeloTTS. We believe that open-source TTS models are essential for the future of AI and are committed to building a decentralized ecosystem to support their development. Yet, the TTS landscape is still in its infancy. In this subnet, we aim to gather the wisdom of the whole open-source community to create the best open-source TTS models.

Roadmap

The MyShell-TTS Subnet is currently in Phase 2 of development. In this phase, we are utilizing the AniSpeech dataset as our source of speaker data. We randomly select one speaker from the dataset, and the goal is to build a TTS model that can perfectly mimic this speaker's voice. This approach allows us to increase our coverage of speakers and scenarios.

For those interested in participating or evaluating our progress:

  • Audio samples from the selected speaker and the text used for evaluation can be found in the `tts_rater` folder of our repository.
  • Options to download and preprocess the dataset for training are available in the `preprocess` folder.

As we progress through this phase, we aim to expand our capabilities to cover more speakers and languages, paving the way for the development of fast-clone models in future phases.

Phase 1: Initial release

Initial release of the subnet, including miner and validator functionality. This phase aim to build a comprehensive pipeline for TTS model training and evaluation. We will begin with a fixed speaker.

Phase 2: Increased coverage

Increase the coverage of speaker and conversation pool. We will recurrently update the speaker and language to cover more scenarios.

Phase 3: Fast-clone models

More generally, we can have fast-clone models that can be adapted to new speakers with a small amount of data, e.g., OpenVoice. We will move to fast-clone models in this phase.

For more information, visit the MyShell-TTS GitHub Repository.
