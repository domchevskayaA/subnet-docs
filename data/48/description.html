NextPlace: The real estate valuation subnet on Bittensor. Predict property sales for accurate valuations.

Introduction

Nextplace AI is decentralizing intelligence around housing markets. In a space controlled by monopolies and gatekeepers, Nexplace seeks to provide a democratized network to evaluate home prices for everybody.

AI Powered Analysis

We're sourcing data from the hottest housing markets in the USA. We're pulling data from New York, San Francisco, Seattle, and many, many more. Our subnet Miners use AI models to predict sale price and sale date on millions of houses per day and we score their accuracy, once a home sells.

Nextplace Roadmap

  1. Expand Markets

    Nexplace will progressively add more markets, challenging miners to develop better and more generalizable models. This expansion will provide increased value to Nextplace consumers by offering insights into a wider range of housing markets.

  2. Visualize Sales-to-Listing Data

    The Nexplace website will evolve into a central hub for visualizing different markets, providing valuable information to home buyers and sellers. The initial phase will focus on:

    • Displaying the difference between home prices and actual sales
    • Showcasing top miners' predictions for current listings

  3. Implement "Price My Home" Feature

    Nextplace will introduce a feature allowing users to obtain property value estimates through the website. This process will involve:

    • Forwarding user requests to top-performing miners
    • Generating estimates for users' homes or properties they're interested in purchasing
    • Providing buyers and sellers with a deeper understanding of their transactions and the true value of homes
